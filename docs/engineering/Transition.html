    Transition

    Transition connects two journey elements, e.g. activities and is navigated when execution of the source element is completed and execution of the target element shall begin.

    Transition may define payload - what the source shall pass along the transition to the element. For example, a transition from “Business Analysis” to “Design” may have “Requirements Document” as its payload.
