    Activity

    Activity is an element of a Journey. Activities can be used to describe:

    • Persona journeys in terms of features provided by products.
    • How a product can be used.
    • Services offered by engineers.

    Because journey is a sub-type of activity a service offered by an engineer may be documented as a journey which references product features and services offered by other engineers. And persona journeys can also include both self-service activities, which use features, and “service” activities where the persona interacts with engineers.
